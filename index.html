Disclaimer

SwAPP is not affiliated with Ryanair. It does not replace official Ryanair apps such as Ryanair Connect.

SwAPP only helps you find which roster can swap with you — the actual swap must be completed through the official Ryanair Connect app.

Your colleagues' crew codes and phone numbers are stored only on your device (localStorage). No data is sent to any server.
